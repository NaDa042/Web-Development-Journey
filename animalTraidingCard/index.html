Panda

Pandas spend up to half their day eating bamboo because it’s low in nutrients, so they have to eat a lot of it.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Weight: 100–150 kilograms
  • Diet: Mostly bamboo
  • Habitat: Mountain forests in China

The giant panda is a bear that lives in China. It has black and white fur, which makes it easy to recognize. Pandas mostly eat bamboo, which is their favorite food. They are calm and gentle animals. Because there are not many pandas left in the wild, they are very special and need protection.
